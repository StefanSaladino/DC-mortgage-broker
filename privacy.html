1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the website operated for David Colavita, Mortgage Agent Level 2, licence number M23005023, serving clients in Ontario, Canada.

This policy applies to personal information collected through this website, email, telephone calls, website forms, analytics tools, advertising tools, future CRM workflows, and other direct communications with David Colavita.

Depending on how you interact with the website, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, information generated through your website activity, and information created through follow-up communications.

Information you voluntarily provide

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Your phone number.
  • Your general location or city.
  • Your mortgage goals, such as buying, renewing, refinancing, investing, or self-employed borrowing.

Website and technical information

  • Browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate device characteristics.
  • General website usage data, such as pages viewed, buttons clicked, and time spent on the website.
  • Referral source, such as whether you arrived from Google Search, a Google Ad, social media, or another website.
  • Approximate location inferred from technical information, such as IP address.
  • Cookie identifiers, advertising identifiers, analytics identifiers, or similar browser-based identifiers where enabled.

You should not submit highly sensitive information through a basic website contact form, including Social Insurance Numbers, banking passwords, full account numbers, government ID numbers, or detailed financial documents unless David specifically provides a secure method for doing so.

3. Website forms and Netlify Forms

This website may use Netlify Forms to collect and process form submissions. When you submit a form, the information you provide may be transmitted to and stored through Netlify’s form handling infrastructure.

Netlify Forms may process submissions for operational purposes, including spam detection, form storage, form management, email notifications, and integrations such as webhooks. Netlify form submissions may also be filtered by spam detection tools before being treated as verified submissions.

Information submitted through a form may be reviewed by David Colavita or authorized service providers helping operate the website, respond to inquiries, manage leads, or support mortgage-related communications.

If a form submission is connected to a marketing source, such as a Google Ad or analytics campaign, the submission may also be associated with campaign information so David can understand which marketing efforts generated the inquiry.

If you submit a form, email David, call David, request a consultation, click a call or email link, or otherwise make an inquiry, your information may be added to David’s customer relationship management system or lead management tools.

The CRM may be used to:

  • Record the source of your inquiry, such as organic search, direct visit, referral, or advertising campaign.

You may request that David stop contacting you or remove your information from active marketing or lead follow-up lists, subject to legal, regulatory, professional, or legitimate business recordkeeping requirements.

This website may use Google Analytics or similar analytics tools to understand how visitors use the website, measure performance, improve page content, identify popular sections, detect technical issues, and understand which marketing channels are generating website visits or inquiries.

Analytics information is generally used in aggregate or pseudonymous form to improve the website and marketing performance. It is not intended to collect sensitive mortgage application details through analytics tracking.

If analytics tools are enabled, visitors may be presented with a privacy or cookie preference banner that allows them to accept or decline optional analytics tracking where required or appropriate.
